## Section 4: Features

**Headline:**
Everything your content team needs to make smarter decisions.

### Feature 1: Topic Discovery Engine
**Headline:** Find what to write next — backed by data.
**Body:** ContentIntel analyses search volume, keyword difficulty, competitor content, and trending topics to surface high-opportunity content ideas every week. Each recommendation comes with an AI-generated angle and supporting keywords, so your team can move straight to writing.

### Feature 2: Content Health Monitor
**Headline:** Catch declining content before it costs you traffic.
**Body:** Automatically tracks every published page against Search Console and GA4 data. Flags content with dropping rankings, falling traffic, low click-through rates, or stale publish dates — and tells you exactly what to do about it.

### Feature 3: On-Demand Topic Validator
**Headline:** Got a topic idea? Validate it in seconds.
**Body:** Paste a keyword or a news article link. ContentIntel returns a full brief: search volume, competition, SERP landscape, competitor analysis, recommended angles, and a suggested outline. Know whether a topic is worth pursuing before you commit.

### Feature 4: Multi-Domain Support
**Headline:** One dashboard for all your websites.
**Body:** Manage multiple domains from a single workspace. Switch between properties with a dropdown — each domain has its own data, competitors, and recommendations. Perfect for agencies and multi-brand teams.

### Feature 5: Approval Workflow
**Headline:** From recommendation to assignment in two clicks.
**Body:** AI suggests topics. Your content lead reviews them. Your marketing lead approves. Writers get assigned. Simple statuses, full audit trail, no spreadsheet chaos.

### Feature 6: No Vendor Lock-In
**Headline:** Your tools, your data.
**Body:** ContentIntel connects to the platforms you already use — DataforSEO, SEMrush, Google Search Console, GA4, HubSpot. Bring your own API keys. Switch providers any time. Your data stays yours.

## Tone & Style Notes for Stitch

- **Tone:** Professional but not corporate. Direct, confident, no fluff. Think "smart colleague explaining a tool," not "enterprise sales deck."
- **Visual direction:** Clean, modern, data-forward. Think dashboard screenshots, metric cards, before/after comparisons. Avoid generic stock photos of people at laptops.
- **Colour suggestions:** Dark navy or charcoal primary, with an accent colour (teal, electric blue, or warm amber) for CTAs and highlights. The dashboard itself should feel like a premium analytics tool.
- **Typography:** Sans-serif throughout. Clean hierarchy — large bold headlines, lighter body text.
- **Illustrations/Graphics:**
  - Hero: Could show a stylised dashboard with topic cards and scores
  - How it works: Minimal icons or line illustrations for each step
  - Features: Small UI mockups or screenshots (can be created after dashboard is built)
- **Social proof:** Leave space for testimonials and logos — these will be populated during/after beta
- **Mobile:** The page should work well on mobile. The waitlist form should be prominent and easy to complete on a phone.
